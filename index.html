HC‑P14 High Speed Bolt Pointer

High-speed bolt chamfering and pointing machine

Overview

The HC‑P14 bolt chamfer machine is designed to overcome the limitations of traditional lathe or die chamfering processes. By combining high capacity, efficiency and flexibility, it delivers consistent chamfering and pointing for bolts and screws.

Key Features

  • Processes screws and bolts with diameters between 3–15 mm and lengths of 12‑150 mm.
  • Handles various special bolt shapes.
  • Quick tool change for reduced downtime.
  • Stable control powered by servo motors.
  • Cost‑effective compared to similar European models.
