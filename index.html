Overview

The HC‑P14 bolt chamfer machine is designed to overcome the limitations of traditional lathe or die chamfering processes by increasing production capacity, efficiency and flexibility. It handles a wide range of screws and bolts【24436081102947†L118-L137】.

Key Features

  • Processes screws and bolts with diameters between 3–15 mm and lengths of 12–150 mm【24436081102947†L118-L137】.
  • Handles various special bolt shapes【24436081102947†L118-L137】.
  • Quick tool change for reduced downtime【24436081102947†L118-L137】.
  • Stable control powered by servo motors【24436081102947†L160-L176】.
