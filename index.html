High‑Speed Bolt
Pointer HC‑P14

Product Overview

The HC‑P14 is a high‑performance bolt pointing solution engineered for manufacturers seeking greater efficiency and precision in finishing bolt ends. Its robust construction and high‑speed processing capabilities make it ideal for a variety of industrial applications.

Specifications

  • Diameter range: q2 – 10 mm
  • Length range: 10 – 150 mm

Applications

The HC‑P14 is versatile and suited for multiple industries. It is particularly well suited for use in automotive and aerospace manufacturing, but can also be adapted for other fastener production requirements.

Features

  • High processing speed for increased productivity
  • Robust design ensuring reliability and durability
  • Easy tooling changeover for minimal downtime
  • Precise finishing of bolt points
